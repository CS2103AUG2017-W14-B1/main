About Us

iContacts was developed by a team of students, with assistance from the Lecturer and Project Supervisor, as a project for the module CS2103T.

It is built upon the AddressBook - Level 4 project developed by the se-edu team.

We are a team based in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore.
